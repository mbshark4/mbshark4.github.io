Problem statement: Grubhub Wait Times

Frequent users of of delevery services like Grubhub encounter the problem of inaccurate delivery time estimates, which can lead to frustration and incenvenience. These inaccurate wait times can disrupt their meal planning, cause delays in their schedules, and affect their overall dining experience. There is a need for a solution that provides reliable and real-time delivery time estimates to enhance the convenience and satisfaction of ordering food online.
